About Me

Hi, I'm Tula Rajbanshi, a compassionate veterinarian dedicated to empowering animal welfare. Over the past five years, I've rescued and treated street animals in Nepal, founding Let's Care Nepal and Salin Nepal to make a difference. Despite facing challenges like limited resources and resistance, my unwavering commitment remains strong.


I've rescued 3200 street dogs across various districts, witnessing their dire conditions firsthand. Educating communities on rabies prevention and advocating for sterilization programs, I aim to create lasting change.


Looking ahead, my vision is to establish a hospital providing free treatment to street animals, ensuring they receive the care they deserve. Join me on this transformative journey, where every paw counts towards a future where animals thrive with love and compassion.
